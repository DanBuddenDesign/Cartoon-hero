Judge Anderson

About Anderson

Judge Cassandra Anderson is a member of the PSI Division of the Justice department of Mega-City One. Like her namesake from antique myth, she has psychic powers. In additions to being able to sense approaching danger and foresee events of the near future, she is also telepathic. This allows her to read other people's minds and use mental attacks.

Anderson's Bio

  1. As a young child, her abilities attracted the attention of PSI Division, a branch of Justice Department.
  2. Originally appearing while the city was facing the first attack by Judge Death of the Dark Judges, she was instrumental in stopping the attack
  3. Over time, Anderson has become a prominent member of Psi Division, although she has become critical of the weaknesses evident in the Judicial system of Mega-City One
