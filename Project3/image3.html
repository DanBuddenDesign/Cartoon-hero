3d Illustration


Client: Davies Turner


Outline: Davies Turner are a mid sized freight operator based in the UK with multiple drivers. I was approached to create a driver awareness campaign. The training involved online training and hence the keyboard design utilising common driving icons as seen on dashboards. As I wanted an isographic perspective I had to use 3d models to render out the vehicles sat upon the Shift key.


Skills: Retouching, digital painting and colour correction.


Art Direction and 3d illustration: Daniel Arthur Budden
