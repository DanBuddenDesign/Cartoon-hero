Logo design


Client: Various

Outline: I was given a rough visual and asked to create a high res piece of artwork that could be utilised across print and digital media; from large scale monolith posters to social media posts and press ads. I recreated the floor and reflections, graded the lighting so it was convincing, retouched the background and created the particle effects behind the ball.

Skills: Retouching, digital painting and colour correction.

The client needed a fun identity that reflected retro gaming, with a Back to the Future mad scientist feel. I used custom fonts to add contrast and bold outline to echo the vintage look
