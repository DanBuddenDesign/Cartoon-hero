Design for Print


Client: Royal Shakespeare Company


Outline: 2016 marked the 400th anniversary of Shakespeare's death. To commemorate the occasion, the RSC in Stratford-upon-Avon, embarked on an extraordinary series of work.This 144 page soft back, perfect bound book celebrates this remarkable year with exquisite photography and stories from behind the scenes.


Skills: Layout, graphic design, Indesign.


Art direction: Andy Williams

Graphic designer: Daniel Arthur Budden

Set in Futura font throughout and using a clean and simple grid The Big Year is a memento of an exceptional year and a fascinating view of life at the RSC. I worked with the Art Director to design the complete layout including retouching and photo research.
