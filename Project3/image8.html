Retouching


Client: Spanbild, From homes, sheds, and garages, to commercial and re-locatable buildings, Spanbild offers exceptional design, engineering and construction processes, and the experience of local teams to get the job done.


Outline:They approached me to create a series of insitu visualisations to show off their key products. After an initial consultation I was briefed to put together visualisations that appealed to the New Zealand and Australian market. I was sent the raw CAD files from Spanbild's engineering department and used OTOYs excellent Octane render within Blender combined with HDRI lighting. Final Composition put together in Photoshop.


Skills: 3d modelling, 3d rendering, Retouching
